Our Story

Born at a gaming conference. Built for every industry.

OneHazel was born at a gaming conference — not from a pitch deck, but from the same conversation repeated dozens of times: "We know the tools exist. We just don't have the engineering capacity to integrate them."

Every operator we spoke to was fighting the same battle: months building custom connectors, vendor APIs breaking overnight, engineering teams buried in maintenance. The frustration was universal.

So we built OneHazel. Our AI agents generate production connectors in minutes, monitor them continuously, and repair them automatically when APIs change.
