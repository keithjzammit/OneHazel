FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every new account gets a 14-day free trial with full platform access — including 50,000 API calls, 50 AI agent actions, and the visual workflow builder. No credit card required to start.

What counts as an "API call"?

One API call equals one request routed through the OneHazel gateway to any connected vendor. Webhook deliveries, internal platform calls, and AI agent orchestration calls do not count against your API call quota.

What is an "AI agent action"?

An AI agent action is one run of a specialized agent (DiscoveryAgent, BuilderAgent, ValidationAgent, etc.) against a task. Building a new connector from scratch typically uses 4–6 agent actions. Ongoing self-healing checks are counted separately and at a lower rate.

Can I switch plans mid-month?

Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. You can switch between monthly and annual billing at renewal time.

What happens if I exceed my quota?

We'll notify you at 80% and 100% usage. You can purchase top-up packs at any time without waiting for the next billing cycle. We don't cut off your service without warning.

What is the Founding Customer Programme?

Early beta participants who convert to a paid annual plan at our production launch will lock in 50% off their plan price for life — for as long as they maintain an active subscription. The number of founding spots is limited.
