The Intelligence Layer for Your Industry

OneHazel is a Vertical iPaaS (Integration Platform as a Service) purpose-built for regulated industries. We abstract the complexity of connecting to dozens of specialized AI vendors into a single, unified API.

What is a Vertical iPaaS?

Traditional iPaaS platforms (like Zapier or Mulesoft) are horizontal—they connect generic tools (CRMs, email, databases) across many industries.

A Vertical iPaaS is laser-focused on a single industry. OneHazel specializes in industry-specific intelligence—fraud detection, compliance, risk management, analytics, payment intelligence, and operational automation.

Think of us as the "Stripe for Data Intelligence" – one integration unlocks access to an entire ecosystem of specialized AI vendors.
