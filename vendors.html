FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does OneHazel build my connector?

Our AI agents ingest your API documentation, analyse endpoints, authentication methods, and data schemas. They generate production-grade integration code, run automated test suites, and deploy the connector to the marketplace. The typical build takes 3--7 days.

What do I need to provide?

Your API documentation (OpenAPI/Swagger spec, REST docs, or equivalent) and access credentials for a sandbox/testing environment. That is it. No engineering resources from your team are required.

What is the "Verified" badge?

A trust signal shown on your marketplace listing. Verified vendors receive priority maintenance, faster self-healing SLAs, and featured placement in their category. Operators are more likely to connect with Verified vendors.

What happens when I update my API?

Our self-healing AI agents monitor your connector continuously. When they detect API changes such as new endpoints, modified schemas, or updated authentication, they automatically patch the integration. On the Verified tier, this is guaranteed within 24 hours. On Strategic and Partner tiers, within 4 hours.

Can I have multiple connectors for different product tiers?

Yes, on the Strategic and Partner tiers. For example, if you offer Basic, Pro, and Enterprise versions of your product, each can have its own connector with appropriate data mappings and feature sets.

What analytics do I get?

Analytics features are coming soon. Listed vendors will see how many operators have connected. Verified vendors will get a full usage dashboard including API call volume per operator and connection trends. Strategic vendors will additionally receive anonymised operator interest signals. Partners will get revenue attribution reporting.

What is the Partner Programme?

A revenue-share model for vendors who want a deeper relationship with OneHazel. Partners pay no fixed monthly fee. Instead, they share a percentage of revenue generated when operators use their connector. Partners receive all Strategic-tier features plus co-selling, joint go-to-market campaigns, brand association, and product influence. Apply through the contact page to learn more.

Is there a commitment period?

Listed and Verified tiers are month-to-month with no minimum commitment. Strategic requires a 3-month minimum. Partner Programme requires a 12-month commitment.

Can I upgrade between tiers?

Yes. You can move from Listed to Verified or Strategic at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. To join the Partner Programme, you need to apply separately regardless of your current tier.

Do you offer annual billing for vendors?

Yes. Annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly on all paid tiers. Annual plans are billed upfront for the full year.
