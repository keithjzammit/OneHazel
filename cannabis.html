The Integration Gap in Cannabis

Cannabis operators face integration challenges that no existing platform addresses.

Zero Generic Support

Zapier and Make.com have no connectors for METRC, BioTrackTHC, Dutchie, Treez, LeafLink, or any cannabis-specific platform. The industry is invisible to horizontal automation tools, leaving operators to build everything custom or operate manually.

Per-State Compliance

A multi-state operator running dispensaries in 5 states needs 5 different track-and-trace integrations, 5 different tax calculation rules, and 5 different compliance reporting formats. Any lag between POS and track-and-trace risks regulatory violations.

Fragile, Manual Processes

When track-and-trace APIs malfunction — which happens regularly — entire retail operations halt. Manual data entry between systems increases error rates and creates audit liability that can cost licenses.
