FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I change plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated.

Is there a free trial?

The Starter plan is free forever -- no credit card required. Use it as long as you need to evaluate OneHazel.

What counts as an execution?

Each time a workflow runs counts as one execution, regardless of the number of steps or connectors involved.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes. Contact us for annual billing discounts. Annual plans typically save 15-20% compared to monthly billing.

What payment methods do you accept?

All billing is powered by Stripe. We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.
