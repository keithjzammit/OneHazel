Our Story

Born from Frustration

OneHazel was born at a gaming conference. Not from a pitch deck or a whiteboard session, but from the same conversation repeated dozens of times across the exhibition floor: "We know the tools exist. We just don't have the engineering capacity to integrate them."

Every operator we spoke to was fighting the same battle. Months spent building custom connectors. Vendor APIs changing without warning and breaking integrations overnight. Engineering teams buried in maintenance instead of building product. The frustration was universal, and the cost was enormous.

We built OneHazel to make that problem disappear. Our AI agents read vendor documentation, generate production-ready connectors, test them, and monitor them for changes. What used to take an engineering team months now takes minutes. And when an API changes, the platform detects it and repairs the connector automatically.

No more integration debt. No more vendor lock-in. No more 2am fires because a provider updated their API.

What We're Solving

The Problem with Enterprise Integration

Integrations are invisible plumbing. Nobody's customers care how many connectors are running behind the scenes, but when one breaks, everyone notices.

The traditional approach is unsustainable: hire engineers, read documentation, build bespoke code, maintain it forever, and repeat for every new vendor. It doesn't scale, and it pulls your best people away from work that actually moves your business forward.

OneHazel replaces that entire cycle with a single platform. You connect once to OneHazel, and through our unified API you can reach any vendor in our marketplace using the same format, the same data model, the same monitoring dashboard. We handle the authentication, the field mapping, the error handling, and the retries.

One integration. Every vendor. That's it.

Why Companies Trust OneHazel

Security and Reliability by Design

Your data stays yours

Every operator's data is completely isolated through row-level security. No other company on the platform can see your connections, logs, or events. We never store request or response payloads permanently.

Security built in, not bolted on

API keys are hashed with SHA-256 and never stored in plain text. Provider credentials are encrypted with AES-256-GCM at rest. All traffic runs over HTTPS/TLS. Every action is recorded in an immutable audit trail that cannot be deleted or tampered with.

Integrations that heal themselves

Our AI agents continuously monitor connected APIs for changes. When a vendor updates their documentation or modifies an endpoint, OneHazel detects the drift and repairs the connector automatically, before it affects your operations.

Resilience by design

Circuit breakers and rate limiting are built into the gateway, so a misbehaving provider cannot cascade failures into your other integrations. If a vendor degrades, we isolate the problem and alert you in real time.

Compliance-ready from day one

Full GDPR data export and account anonymisation are built into the platform. Your data, your rules.
