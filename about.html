Our Story

Born from Frustration

OneHazel was born from frustration. Every enterprise team we worked with was drowning in integration debt — custom connectors breaking, vendor lock-in, months wasted on pipes instead of product. We built OneHazel to make that problem disappear. Our AI agents generate production connectors in minutes, not months, and our visual workflow builder lets anyone automate without code.
