FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Who qualifies as a platform provider?

Any company that serves multiple operators through a shared platform -- white-label solutions, aggregation platforms, managed services providers, or any business where multiple end-clients operate on your infrastructure.

How is this different from an Enterprise plan?

The Enterprise plan is for a single organisation managing their own integrations. The Platform Provider programme is for companies that serve a network of operators and want to offer integration capabilities across that entire network under one agreement with centralised billing and multi-tenant architecture.

Do my operators need their own OneHazel accounts?

No. Operators access the integration marketplace through your platform. Each operator gets an isolated workspace, but everything is managed under your platform provider agreement. No separate subscriptions, no separate billing.

Can I white-label the marketplace?

White-label embedding is on our product roadmap. Today, operators access the OneHazel marketplace through the platform directly, with accounts provisioned and managed via your API. Contact us to discuss your requirements and timeline.

How does pricing work?

Platform provider pricing is volume-based and tailored to your network size. One agreement covers all operators. The more operators you bring, the lower the per-operator cost. Contact our platform team for a proposal.

How long does deployment take?

Operator accounts can be provisioned via API in minutes. The timeline for your overall deployment depends on how many vendor connectors your operators need -- existing marketplace connectors are available immediately, and new connectors are built by our AI agents in 3--7 days. We are still in early beta for platform provider partnerships, so we will work closely with you to shape the experience.

What happens when I add new operators?

New operators are provisioned instantly through the API or your platform dashboard. They immediately get access to the full vendor marketplace with their own isolated workspace. No additional setup from OneHazel's side.

Can my operators request specific vendors?

Yes. Operators can request any vendor not yet on the marketplace. Our AI agent pipeline builds new connectors in minutes, not weeks -- the connector is reviewed, tested, and added to the marketplace for immediate activation. This is included in the platform provider agreement.
